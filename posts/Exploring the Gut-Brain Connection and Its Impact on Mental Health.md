Most people never connect their digestive health to their emotional well-
being, but the science is clear. Nearly 95% of your body’s serotonin is
produced in your gut, not your brain. If you’ve been struggling with anxiety,
low mood, or mental fog alongside digestive issues, this may be the root-cause
connection you’ve been missing. Learn how the gut-brain axis works, what
disrupts it, and the evidence-based nutrition strategies that can help you
feel better from the inside out.
